	<h1 style="color:aliceblue; font-family: Cambria, 'Hoefler Text', 'Liberation Serif', Times, 'Times New Roman', 'serif'">The Fool</h1>

	<p style="color:#F4F9E9; font-family: 'Gill Sans', 'Gill Sans MT', 'Myriad Pro', 'DejaVu Sans Condensed', Helvetica, Arial, 'sans-serif'">In Tarot, there are two categories of Arcana: The Major Arcana and The Minor Arcana. In The Major Arcana, the first Arcana is The Fool. Represented by the number 0, The Fool symbolizes innocence and the beginning of a journey. When doing a reading, the card either shows upright or reversed. Upright, The Fool represents potential, new beginnings, opportunity, and adventure. Reversed, it representes carelessness, negligence, uncertainty, and apathy. Back at <a href="https://trinity-mclaren.github.io/NewWebsite/;" style="color:aliceblue">The Storm</a> I mentioned that we are all The Fool, especially at this point in life and in many other points. This is because the current pandemic has opened a new chapter in our lives, requiring us to begin a new journey.
